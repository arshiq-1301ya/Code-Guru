Government Leather Institute Agra Details

Government Leather Institute (GLI), Agra, established in 1962, is one of India's premier government institutes specializing in leather and footwear technology education. Affiliated with the Board of Technical Education, Uttar Pradesh (BTEUP), the institute offers industry-oriented diploma programs in Computer Science & Engineering, Leather Technology (Tanning), and Leather Technology Footwear (CASD). GLI provides modern laboratories, well-equipped workshops, experienced faculty, library facilities, and practical training to develop technical skills and prepare students for successful careers in the leather, footwear, and information technology industries.
