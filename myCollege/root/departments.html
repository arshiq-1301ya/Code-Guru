Departments

Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science offers a strong foundation in programming, data structures, algorithms, and modern technologies like web development and artificial intelligence. Students get access to well-equipped computer labs and guidance for projects, internships, and placements.

HOD: Mr.Rk Singh

Last updated: 2025

leather Technology Tanning

Leather Technology (Tanning) focuses on transforming raw hides and skins into high-quality finished leather using scientific and industrial tanning processes. The course covers leather processing, chemical treatment, quality testing, environmental practices, and modern tannery operations with extensive practical training in well-equipped laboratories and workshops.

HOD: Mr.Dn Swami

Last updated: 2025

Footware Technology (CSAD)

Leather Technology Footwear (Computer Aided Shoe Design - CASD) prepares students for the footwear industry by teaching shoe design, pattern making, computer-aided design (CAD), footwear manufacturing, quality control, and leather goods production. Students receive hands-on training using modern equipment and software to meet current industry standards.

HOD: Prof. Suresh Kumar

Last updated: 2025
