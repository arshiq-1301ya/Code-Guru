Student Life in Government Leather Institute Agra

Life at Government Leather Institute Agra goes far beyond the classroom. We offer students a vibrant environment to learn, grow, and explore their interests through academics, skill-building activities, festivals, and real-world industry exposure.

Academic Flexibility

  • Choice-based credit system allowing students to pick electives of their interest.
  • Flexible class timings for students involved in sports or extracurricular activities.
  • Option to switch minor subjects between semesters based on interest.

Skill Development

  • Regular workshops on coding, communication, and personality development.
  • Seminars conducted by industry experts and alumni.
  • Events like hackathons, debates, and quiz competitions to sharpen practical skills.

Festivals and Events

  • Annual cultural fest featuring music, dance, and drama competitions.
  • Tech fest showcasing student innovations, robotics, and coding contests.
  • Sports day with inter-department tournaments and athletic events.

Internships and Industry Exposure

  • Tie-ups with companies for summer and semester internships.
  • Placement training sessions including resume building and mock interviews.
  • Guest lectures from industry professionals to bridge the gap between academics and the workplace.
